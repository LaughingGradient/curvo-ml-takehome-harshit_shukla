# Handling Uncertainty

When someone sounds unsure or hesitant, focus on reducing ambiguity.

## Goals
- Surface the missing information
- Provide clarity or evidence
- Suggest low-risk experiments

## Strategy
1. Ask what information is missing
2. Offer examples, data, or comparisons
3. Suggest a small test or trial

## Example phrases
- “What would help you feel more confident?”
- “Would a quick test help clarify?”
- “Is there something specific you’re unsure about?”
