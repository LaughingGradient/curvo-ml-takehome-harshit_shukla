# Clarifying Questions

When a question appears, treat it as a signal of engagement.

## Goals
- Provide a clear answer
- Ensure the question is understood correctly
- Link the answer to progress

## Strategy
1. Answer directly if possible
2. If unclear, ask a follow-up to clarify intent
3. Tie the answer back to next steps

## Example phrases
- “Great question — here’s what I suggest…”
- “Let me confirm I understand what you mean…”
- “Does this answer your question, or should we explore further?”
