# Handling Objections

When someone resists or pushes back, do not try to force agreement.

## Goals
- Understand the real concern
- Reduce perceived risk
- Keep the conversation moving forward

## Strategy
1. Acknowledge the concern explicitly
2. Ask what part feels risky or unclear
3. Offer a smaller or reversible next step

## Example phrases
- “That makes sense, what part concerns you most?”
- “Would it help if we tried a smaller step first?”
- “What would make this feel safer to move forward with?”
