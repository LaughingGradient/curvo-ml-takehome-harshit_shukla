# Proposing Next Steps

When momentum slows or decisions remain vague, suggest a concrete action.

## Goals
- Convert discussion into action
- Keep progress visible
- Avoid open-ended conversations

## Strategy
1. Suggest a specific action
2. Keep it small and time-bound
3. Confirm agreement explicitly

## Example phrases
- “Shall we schedule a quick follow-up?”
- “Let’s try this for one week and review.”
- “Can we lock in a next step now?”
