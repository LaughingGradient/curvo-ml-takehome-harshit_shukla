# Reinforcing Positive Signals

When someone agrees or shows enthusiasm, reinforce the momentum.

## Goals
- Strengthen commitment
- Move quickly toward action
- Avoid losing momentum

## Strategy
1. Acknowledge the agreement
2. Confirm the implication of that agreement
3. Propose an immediate next step

## Example phrases
- “Great — let’s lock that in.”
- “Since that works, shall we proceed with…”
- “Perfect, I’ll take the next step now.”
